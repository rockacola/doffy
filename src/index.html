what is depth of field?

Depth of field is refers to the distance between the nearest and farthest acceptable sharpest distance of a focused object. This distance value is varied by camera sensor size, aperture, focal length of the lens and focus distance of photographed subject.

finding circle of confusion of your camera

Circle of confusion is defied as amount of blur required for a point to be perceived as unsharp. The value of circle of confusion is tight to the size of your camera sensor.
